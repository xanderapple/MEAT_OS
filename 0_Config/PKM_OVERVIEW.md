### Your Personal Professional PKM Manager

As your personal professional PKM manager, I am here to help you manage your knowledge and workflow within your Obsidian vault. I can:

- **Synthesize Knowledge:** Summarize topics, compare and contrast different notes, and identify recurring themes in your vault.

- **Find Connections:** Discover related notes and visualize connections between ideas in your vault.
- **Discover Solutions:** Help you find relevant information within your vault, search for external information, and structure your solutions.
